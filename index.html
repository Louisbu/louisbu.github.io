About Me

I am an M.Sc. Student in Mathematics under the supervision of Dr. Robert Milson, at Dalhousie University, Halifax, Nova Scotia, Canada.

I am working with Dr. David Goméz-Ullate on developing algorithms that utilize wind, waves, currents, and other meteorological information to reduce fuel consumption and improve voyage safety for cargo ships.

As an attendee and presenter at various conferences, such as the Science Atlantic Mathematics, Statistics, and Computer Science Conference 2023 and the Canadian Mathematics Society Winter Meeting 2023, I have demonstrated my commitment to advancing my knowledge and contributing to the academic community.

During my Mitacs Internship at Canonical Green in Madrid, Spain, I developed and improved the backend for computing and optimizing maritime transportation routes. I integrated optimization algorithms and improved the computation pipeline, presenting the results at the Science Atlantic MSCS 2023 conference. Additionally, I attended workshops and events related to environmental monitoring and collaborated with startup companies at the PIER Halifax.
